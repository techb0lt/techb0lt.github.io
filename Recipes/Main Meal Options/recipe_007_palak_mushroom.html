    Paalak Mushroom

    • Key Stats¶

    • Ingredients¶

      1. Avocado Oil
     - 1 tbsp in step 1
2. Red Onion
     - 150 gms(Large) in step 1
3. Ginger
     - 1 tsp in step 1
4. Garlic
     - 4 cloves in step 1
5. Green Chilli
     - 1 tsp(1 to 2 chillies) in step 3
6. Cumin Seeds
     - 1 tsp in step 4
7. Mustard Seeds
     - 1 tsp in step 4
8. Baby Spinach
     - 300 gms in step 5
9. Mushrooms
     - 400 gms in step 6
10. Pink Himalayan Salt
     - to taste
11. Garam Masala
     - 1 tsp in step 8
12. Amchoor Powder
     - 1 tsp in step 8

    • Cookwares¶

      1. Spatula
      2. Knife
      3. Saucepan
      4. Medium Sized Pan

    • Steps¶

      • 1: Heat 1 tbsp Avocado oil in a medium sized pan. Finely chop with knife and add 150 gms(Large) red onion, 1 tsp ginger and 4 cloves garlic.
      • 2: Mix with spatula and saut for 3 minutes.
      • 3: Then finely chop 1 tsp(1 to 2 chillies) green chilli and add to the saucepan.
      • 4: Now add 1 tsp cumin seeds and 1 tsp mustard seeds.
      • 5: Add fresh 300 gms baby spinach and cook for 5 minutes.
      • 6: Now add 400 gms mushrooms and Pink Himalayan Salt (to taste).
      • 7: Cook for a further 3 minutes.
      • 8: Add 1 tsp garam masala and 1 tsp amchoor powder.
      • 9: Continue cooking for another 3-4 minutes.
      • 10: Serve hot.
